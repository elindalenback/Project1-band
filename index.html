About the band

Welcome to the musical world of Rolling Echoes! We are a dynamic and genre-defying band, blending the rich storytelling of country music with the raw energy of indie rock and the intimate melodies of singer-songwriters.

Our Journey:

The journey of Rolling Echoes began with a shared passion for creating music that resonates with the soul. Each member brings a unique flavor to the sound, resulting in a harmonious fusion that transcends traditional boundaries.

Musical Fusion:

Our sound is a tapestry of emotions, weaving through the landscapes of country heartbreak, indie anthems, and introspective singer-songwriter narratives. With each note, we invite you to join us on a sonic adventure that explores the vast spectrum of human experience.

The Band Members

Jackson "Jax" Steele

Guitarist

Jax is the lead guitarist, infusing the band's sound with his masterful guitar work. Known for his expressive solos and intricate melodies, Jax brings a rock 'n' roll spirit to Rolling Echoes. His stage energy and love for the blues contribute to the band's dynamic sound.

Liam Stone

Singer

Liam is the charismatic frontman and lead vocalist of Rolling Echoes. With a voice that effortlessly transitions from soulful croons to powerful anthems, Liam's stage presence is magnetic. His lyrics delve into the highs and lows of life, creating a profound connection with the audience.

Max Drummond

Drummer

Max is the heartbeat of Rolling Echoes, commanding the rhythm with precision and passion. As the drummer, Max's powerful beats and creative fills propel the band's energy on stage. Drawing inspiration from various genres, Max's drumming style is both versatile and driving.

Owen Rivers

Guitarist

Owen is the rhythm guitarist, providing the sonic foundation for Rolling Echoes. His skillful chord progressions and harmonies add depth to the band's music. Owen's style blends elements of folk and indie rock, creating a rich tapestry of sound.

Join the Journey:

Whether you're a dedicated fan or a newcomer to our sound, we invite you to join the journey. Follow us on social media, explore our music, and be part of the Rolling Echoes community. Thank you for being part of our musical adventure. Let the echoes roll on!
